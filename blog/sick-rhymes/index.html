Sick rhymes

  • May 23, 2008
  • 1 min of reading
  • Stats

This article has been published 15 years ago. If you're into internet archeology, read it — but at your own risk. Honza doesn't have to necessarily agree with the content by now.

Color is there
to make two socks
different.

Time is there
to let people to be
late.

Rules are there
to be broken by
rebels.

Rhymes are there
to be garbled by
me.
