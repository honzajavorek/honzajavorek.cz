Brain waves of the friend

  • Nov 2, 2007
  • Honza Javorek

Warning! This article has been published 15 years ago. If you're into internet archeology, read it — but at your own risk. Honza doesn't have to necessarily agree with the content by now.

Listening emotive song,
while whole night is so sad,
wishing something wrong,
and hoping for something bad.

She's so lovely and so smart…
Her charm… Touches my heart!

But!

The point comes,
to break these rhymes…

She's forbidden.
Just not available…

The girl loves someone else.
And I think someone worse.

Probably.

So, no emotion… Just slow down…
Show must go on. Show for this clown.

Honza Javorek is a software engineer and a founder of junior.guru, a Czech project helping people to get their first job in tech. Since 2011 he's been helping to drive the growth and success of the Czech Python User Group.
